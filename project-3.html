Climate-resilient food systems in arid regions

This project investigates the ecological dynamics of arid and semi-arid systems, focusing on factors that support long-term ecosystem stability. It will examine vegetation patterns, soil processes, and climate stressors to identify interventions that enhance ecosystem resilience and prevent further degradation.

Summary

This project investigates the ecological dynamics of arid and semi-arid systems, focusing on factors that support long-term ecosystem stability. It will examine vegetation patterns, soil processes, and climate stressors to identify interventions that enhance ecosystem resilience and prevent further degradation.
