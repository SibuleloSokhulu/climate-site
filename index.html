Our Purpose

We prioritise impact-driven science and community-led research, ensuring that knowledge production is grounded in real-world challenges and generates tangible benefits for both society and the environment. Key thematic areas include agricultural vulnerability, ecosystem sustainability, and climate-resilience development.

Our Mission

Develop innovative, context-specific strategies to address agricultural vulnerability, environmental degradation, and food insecurity. Promote equitable and sustainable development through research that serves people and the planet.

Our Goal

Empower young researchers and students, especially from historically disadvantaged backgrounds, through active participation in research projects. Foster partnerships across academia, government, industry, and civil society so that research findings translate into meaningful policy and practical impact.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognised centre of excellence in climate adaptation research, driving sustainable solutions for arid and semi-arid regions while empowering communities through inclusive, transdisciplinary, and impact-driven science.
